I am a Biomedical Engineering graduate from E.G.S Pillay Engineering College (2022–2026), graduating with a CGPA of 9.67. My work bridges the gap between healthcare challenges and engineering solutions — from AI-driven diagnostics to physical medical devices that reach real patients.
