About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineering graduate (2022–2026) from E.G.S Pillay Engineering College, achieving a CGPA of 9.67. My work lives at the intersection of engineering, medicine, and innovation — building devices and systems that make healthcare more accessible, intelligent, and human-centered.
